Story

Set in Detroit in 2038, the city has been transformed thanks to the creation and widespread use of androids. However, things take a dramatic turn when these androids begin to show signs of self-awareness, leading to chaos. Players control three main characters—Kara, Connor, and Markus—who each have their own perspective, goals, and abilities as they navigate a deeply emotional journey. As the story unfolds, the decisions you make as a player will shape their paths, with each character questioning their identity and values along the way. The game tackles complex, mature themes, such as morality and free will, and every choice you make has a direct impact on the events that follow, ultimately determining the characters' fates and the outcome of their cause.
